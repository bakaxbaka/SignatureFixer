Axiom ax-oa6 1030
Description: Orthoarguesian law (6-variable version). (Contributed by NM, 29-Nov-1998.)
Hypotheses
Ref Expression

Assertion
Ref Expression
ax-oa6 (((a ∪ b) ∩ (c ∪ d)) ∩ (e ∪ f)) ≤ (b ∪ (a ∩ (c ∪ (((a ∪ c) ∩ (b ∪ d)) ∩ (((a ∪ e) ∩ (b ∪ f)) ∪ ((c ∪ e) ∩ (d ∪ f)))))))
