Definition df-id4oa 58
Description: The 4-variable orthoarguesian identity term. (Contributed by NM, 28-Nov-1998.)
Assertion
Ref Expression
df-id4oa (a ≡ c, d ≡OA b) = ((a ≡ d ≡OA b) ∪ ((a ≡ d ≡OA c) ∩ (b ≡ d ≡OA c)))
